## Why Install Minipass?

Installing Minipass on your home screen gives you:

- **Quick access** - Open Minipass with one tap, no need to type the URL
- **Full-screen experience** - No browser toolbar, feels like a native app
- **Push notifications** - Receive updates about your activities
- **No app store needed** - Install directly from your browser
- **Minimal storage** - Takes almost no space on your device

## Requirements

| Platform | Browser | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Android | Chrome, Edge, Samsung Internet | Most browsers support PWA |
| iPhone/iPad | **Safari only** | Chrome and Firefox on iOS cannot install PWAs |

## Android - Install

1. Open Minipass in **Chrome** (or Edge/Samsung Internet)
2. Tap the menu (3 dots in top-right) → **"Add to Home screen"**
3. Tap **"Add"** to confirm

The Minipass icon will appear on your home screen.

## Android - Uninstall

1. Long-press the Minipass icon on your home screen
2. Drag to **"Remove"** or tap **"Uninstall"**

## iPhone - Install

> **Important**: You must use **Safari**. Other browsers on iPhone cannot install PWAs.

1. Open Minipass in **Safari**
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow at the bottom of the screen)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** to confirm

The Minipass icon will appear on your home screen.

## iPhone - Uninstall

1. Long-press the Minipass icon on your home screen
2. Tap **"Remove App"** → **"Delete App"**

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Add to Home Screen" option not visible | Make sure you're using HTTPS. Try clearing your browser cache and cookies. |
| iPhone: Option not available | You must use Safari. Chrome/Firefox on iOS cannot install PWAs. |
| App not updating | Close the app completely (swipe it away), then reopen it. |
| Notifications not working | Check your device's notification settings for the browser you used to install. |
| App looks different after update | Clear the app from your home screen and reinstall it. |
